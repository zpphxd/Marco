5. DISTANCE ACCURACY ANALYSIS
----------------------------------------------------------------------

  When phones were side by side (~0-1m actual):
    Landmark estimate: 0.6-1.5m ← accurate!
    RSSI-only:         0.1-0.9m ← also good at close range

  When phones were in different rooms (~5-10m actual):
    Landmark estimate: 5.9-10.0m ← tracks real distance well
    RSSI-only:         6.3-27.5m ← wildly inaccurate at range

  Conclusion: Landmark triangulation provides MUCH more
  stable distance estimates than raw RSSI, especially at
  medium to long range where RSSI becomes unreliable.
